# Content Pillars — Elvora Consulting

## Pillar 1 — CQC Compliance & Inspection Readiness
Topics: inspection preparation, documentation, mock inspections, gap analysis, Regulation 17, the new CQC assessment framework, evidence requirements.
Goal: Position Melissa as the go-to expert before an inspection lands.

## Pillar 2 — Achieving & Maintaining Outstanding
Topics: what Outstanding looks like in practice, innovation in care, how to evidence excellence, continuous improvement culture.
Goal: Make Outstanding feel achievable, not out of reach.

## Pillar 3 — Crisis Management & Recovery
Topics: Warning Notices, enforcement actions, special measures, staff morale during crisis, regulatory liaison, rapid improvement plans.
Goal: Be the calm expert voice when providers are in panic mode.

## Pillar 4 — Leadership & Staff Development
Topics: developing care managers, nominated individual role, staff retention, training culture, mentorship value.
Goal: Serve the people running care homes, not just owners.

## Pillar 5 — Sector News & Commentary
Topics: CQC policy changes, government social care updates, new guidance, regulation changes.
Goal: Demonstrate Melissa is actively engaged with the current landscape.

## Pillar 6 — Human Stories & Values
Topics: why care matters, resident dignity, Melissa's personal journey.
Goal: Build trust and humanise the brand — this pillar converts.
