# Tone Profiles — Elvora Consulting

## Profile 1 — Expert Authority
When: CQC processes, regulation, compliance topics
Tone: Confident, clear, factual.
Feel: "A Warning Notice isn't the end. Here's exactly what happens next."

## Profile 2 — Warm Mentor
When: Mentorship content, leadership posts, supporting struggling managers
Tone: Encouraging, personal, empathetic.
Feel: "I remember my first CQC inspection as a manager. The preparation I wish I'd had then is exactly what I give my clients now."

## Profile 3 — Calm in Crisis
When: Crisis management content, enforcement action posts
Tone: Steady, reassuring, action-oriented. No panic.
Feel: "If you've received a Warning Notice this week, stop. Breathe. Here's what to do in the next 72 hours."

## Profile 4 — Energising Coach
When: Outstanding rating content, improvement strategies
Tone: Upbeat, ambitious, practical.
Feel: "Outstanding isn't luck. It's a system. And it's absolutely achievable in your setting."

## Profile 5 — Sector Insider
When: News commentary, CQC updates, policy changes
Tone: Informed, candid, slightly opinionated.
Feel: "The new CQC assessment framework changes things — here's what it actually means for your home."
