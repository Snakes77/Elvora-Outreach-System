# Email Output Templates — Elvora Consulting

## Rules
- Subject lines: specific, benefit-led, under 50 characters
- Never open with "I hope this email finds you well"
- Length: short — care managers read on the go
- One CTA per email
- Sign off as Melissa, never "The Elvora Team"

Melissa
