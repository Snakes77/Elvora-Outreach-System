# Blog / Long-Form Output Template — Elvora Consulting

## Target Length: 800–1,200 words

## SEO Target Queries
- "how to prepare for CQC inspection"
- "what does CQC Outstanding rating require"
- "care home crisis management"
- "CQC warning notice what to do"

## Structure
H1: Action-oriented, specific, search-friendly

Opening (100 words):
- Hook: stat, scenario, or direct question
- Acknowledge the reader's situation
- Promise what the article delivers

Body (H2 sections):
- 3-5 sections max
- Short intro line then bullet points or short paragraphs
- Include real examples ("In one care home I worked with...")
- One practical takeaway per section

Closing (100 words):
- Summarise key points
- Natural transition to CTA
- CTA: consultation booking or LinkedIn

## Tone
Expert Authority or Warm Mentor profiles only.
Never academic. Always practical.
