## Platform: LinkedIn (primary)

### Post Structure — Expert Tip
Line 1: Bold opener (stat, question, or provocative statement)
Lines 2-3: Context or problem
Lines 4-6: Insight or tip (short paragraphs, line breaks)
Line 7: Takeaway or CTA
Line 8: 3-5 hashtags

### Post Structure — Personal Story
Line 1: Set the scene
Lines 2-4: Specific story
Lines 5-6: Lesson or turning point
Line 7: How it applies to reader today
Line 8: CTA + hashtags

### Post Structure — Sector News Commentary
Line 1: News hook
Lines 2-3: Plain English explanation
Lines 4-5: Melissa's take / what providers should do
Line 6: CTA + hashtags
